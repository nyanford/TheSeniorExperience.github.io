      Recommendations by Irene Nyanford

Career Center

As a suggestion for college students, I highly recommend utilizing the Career Center. This valuable resource can assist you in finding internships and jobs, as well as improving your resume. Personally, I have benefited greatly from using the Career Center, as it has helped me develop my professional skills and make valuable connections.

University Writing Center

The writing center at my university has been an invaluable resource for me when it comes to improving my academic writing. They have assisted me with my papers, presentations, and resume. Furthermore, they offer guidance on how to enhance my writing skills, which has been beneficial for my career growth.

University Writing Center

Academic Student Success Center

The Student Success Center at my academic institution has been instrumental in helping me achieve academic success. I have learned valuable time management skills, received tutoring, and obtained academic coaching through this resource. Moreover, I have been able to connect with other students who are in similar academic situations.
